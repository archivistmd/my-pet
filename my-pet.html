The Fereldan warhound

The pride and joy of Ferelden, the mabari is a loyal and intellegent companion.

Hearders, guards, cuddle with you by the fire, mabari are unrivaled.

Favorite foods

Some of a mabari's favorite food is as followed:

  • Lamb bone
  • Beef bone
  • Fish skins
  • Duck
  • Chicken
  • Randomly found soggy cake (though do avoid when possible

Fun facts

  • They are excellent at finding things, from things you thought you lost to things you didn't know you lost:
    • A good shoe
    • Random socks you misplaced
    • A random soggy cake
  • They are able to understand language even if you can't understand them
  • Mabari bond for life.
  • Mabari can be up to 5'3" tall.

Overall mabari are pretty neat, definately a hound to takewith you around the wilds.
